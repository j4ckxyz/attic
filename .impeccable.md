## Design Context

### Users
- Primary user is the account owner maintaining a personal Bluesky archive.
- Usage context is local-first, utility-driven retrieval: quick scanning, search, and thread lookup.
- Core jobs are syncing posts reliably, finding historical posts quickly, and bookmarking important items.

### Brand Personality
- Practical
- Calm
- Trustworthy

### Aesthetic Direction
- Utilitarian archive interface with warm, low-glare surfaces and restrained emphasis.
- Priority is readability and speed of interaction over visual novelty.
- Keep UI lightweight, familiar, and keyboard-friendly.

### Design Principles
1. Retrieval first: every interaction should reduce time-to-find.
2. Clarity over decoration: hierarchy and labels must be explicit.
3. Stable behavior: controls and navigation remain predictable across views.
4. Accessible by default: keyboard/focus/contrast patterns are baseline requirements.
5. Local tool feel: fast, dependable, minimal cognitive overhead.
